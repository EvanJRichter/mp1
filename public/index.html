Hello! I am Evan Richter.


I am a senior at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Art+Design. My interests range from web development and data visualization to photography and videograpy.


This past summer, I interned at Vodori in Chicago where I worked on enterprise application development, utilizing Java Spring, Angular 2, HapiJS, and more.


The summer before that I worked at Dow Chemical doing web development, and the summer before that I worked at John Deere doing Android Development.


At UIUC I am the web director of Pizza FM, a student run radio station. I designed and built their website and android application.


I am the president of Potted Meat Sketch Comedy where I write, direct, and perform in live and video comedic sketches.


I am a member of the Kappa Chapter of Theta Tau, a co-ed professional engineering fraternity. Last year I was the Public Relations Chair, where I designed web content, print content, and apparel. This year I am the Historian, where I document chapter goings-on


On this website I will be hosting both my technical and artistic projects.
